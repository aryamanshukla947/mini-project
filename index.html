Chocolate Cake

Prep: 20 min Cook: 30 min Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a round cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add water, oil, vinegar, and vanilla. Mix until just combined.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips if using.
  5. Pour batter into pan and bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!
